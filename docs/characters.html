Heroes, Villains, and Weirdos

A fast dossier on the folks who shape Ooo. Each blurb calls out vibes, stakes, and where to look in the Watchlist.

Jump to: Home · Locations · Watchlist

Core duo

  • Finn the Human — earnest hero wrestling with growing up, destiny, and scars from the Lich. Key arcs: Grass Sword lineage, Farmworld fallout, and reflective episodes like "The Comet."
  • Jake the Dog — stretchy trickster with hidden wisdom, a past tied to Warren Ampersand, and a surprisingly tender arc into fatherhood with Lady Rainicorn.
"Mathematical mantra: never do rm -rf /." — Finn the Human

Royalty and rulers

  • Princess Bubblegum (PB) — scientist-queen of the Candy Kingdom; oscillates between benevolent protector and ends-justify-the-means tinkerer. Watch her bond with Marceline and her political chess with the Flame Kingdom.
  • Ice King / Simon Petrikov — tragic heart of the show. The crown grants ice powers and madness; Simon-focused episodes deliver lore and emotional gut punches.
  • Flame Princess — firebender royalty navigating anger and autonomy. Her dynamic with Finn explores honesty, power, and responsibility.

Allies and antiheroes

  • Marceline the Vampire Queen — musician, vampire, survivor of the Mushroom War. Her history with Simon, her dad Hunson, and PB makes her the emotional axis of the series.
  • BMO — living game console and robot child with an unshakable sense of play and identity. BMO episodes often quietly expand the world and themes of loneliness.
  • Lumpy Space Princess (LSP) — chaotic neutral energy. Often comedic relief, sometimes the unexpected savior.

Cosmic threats and catalysts

  • The Lich — entropy embodied, showing up through possession, prophecy, and cosmic doors. Track him via Billy, the snail, and Prismo's time room.
  • Prismo — wish-granting, dimension-bending pal in a cosmic loft. His rules matter to the multiverse and to containing the Lich.
  • The Mushroom War — not a person, but the radioactive past. Relics like the crown, armor, and tech drive many plots.

Relationships that matter

  • Finn & Jake — brotherhood tested by change as Jake starts a family.
  • PB & Marceline — from estranged to openly caring; anchors the theme of chosen family.
  • Simon & Marceline — wartime protector and adopted child; curing the crown revolves around them.

Pick your mood

  • Heart: Simon/Marceline stories, music episodes, and BMO adventures.
  • Action: Lich encounters, Finn's sword arcs, Flame Kingdom politics.
  • Cozy weirdness: Tree Fort hangouts, Choose Goose quests, or cooking with Cinnamon Bun.
