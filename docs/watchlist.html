Watchlist and Arcs

Curated episode groupings so you can vibe, cry, or chase lore without rewatching all ten seasons. Names only; check your favorite streamer for exact season and episode numbers.

Jump to: Home · Characters · Locations

Starter mix (tone-setters)

  • Slumber Party Panic; Trouble in Lumpy Space; The Enchiridion! — the questy, weird baseline.
  • Dungeon; City of Thieves — pure adventure pulp.
  • What Was Missing — music and relationships collide.

Marceline and Simon spine (heart of the show)

  • Memory of a Memory; I Remember You; Simon & Marcy — the crown, care, and trauma.
  • Stakes miniseries — Marceline confronts her vampire past.
  • Betty; Come Along With Me epilogue beats — the cost of trying to fix the crown.

The Lich and cosmic fallout (lore heavy)

  • Mortal Folly / Mortal Recoil — first major Lich showdown.
  • The Lich; Finn the Human; Jake the Dog — wish gone wrong leads to Farmworld.
  • Escape from the Citadel; Wake Up — cosmic prison break with lasting consequences.
  • Crossover; The Comet — multiverse and destiny colliding.

Finn, Flame Princess, and emotional growth

  • Too Young; Burning Low — politics and honesty between PB, FP, and Finn.
  • Frost & Fire; Earth & Water — fallout of manipulation and control.
  • Breezy; Flute Spell — recovery, identity, and moving forward.

Magic, wishes, and side dimensions

  • Dungeon Train — a bottle episode of endless adventure.
  • Death in Bloom; Root Beer Guy — surprising stakes in strange corners of Ooo.
  • Prismo episodes (Jake the Brick, Is That You?, etc.) — cozy cosmic lore.
  • Evergreen — origin story of the crown.

Final stretch and closure

  • Stakes (again) — settle Marceline's arc before endgame.
  • Islands miniseries — answers about humanity and Finn's past.
  • Elements miniseries — kingdoms remixed, stakes raised.
  • Come Along With Me — finale tying threads of family, forgiveness, and hope.
