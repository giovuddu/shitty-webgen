Land of Ooo and Beyond

A map-free tour of the realms that shape Adventure Time. Use it to remember who runs what and why each place matters.

Jump to: Home · Characters · Watchlist

Candy Kingdom

  • Vibe: neon science city-state ruled by Princess Bubblegum.
  • Stakes: security experiments, citizens made of candy, the Gumball Guardians.
  • Watch for: PB's projects tipping from benevolent to risky.
  • Pair with: politics episodes and any time the Gumball Guardians wake up.

Ice Kingdom

  • Vibe: lonely spires of frozen rock, lair of Ice King/Simon.
  • Stakes: the crown's corruption, penguin plots, and Simon's struggle for self.
  • Watch for: crown lore drops and Marceline flashbacks tied to this frozen home base.
  • Pair with: Simon/Marceline flashbacks and crown origin stories.

Fire Kingdom

  • Vibe: magma castles and lava moats; ruled by the Flame King until Flame Princess steps up.
  • Stakes: anger management, monarchy vs. autonomy, and explosive showdowns.
  • Pair with: Finn's emotional growth, PB's strategic meddling, and family power struggles.

Tree Fort

  • Vibe: cozy adventurer HQ for Finn and Jake.
  • Stakes: where gear, swords, mixtapes, and quests begin.
  • Watch for: episodes that start here often swing from chill to dungeon crawl.

Nightosphere

  • Vibe: infernal bureaucracy and soul-sucking contracts; run by Hunson Abadeer.
  • Stakes: Marceline's family ties, deals gone wrong, and heavy metal aesthetics.
  • Pair with: music-heavy episodes and rebellions against dad's rules.

Prismo's Time Room

  • Vibe: cosmic loft outside time, guarded by the Divine Psycho.
  • Stakes: wish rules, multiverse branches, and Lich containment.
  • Watch for: any Prismo appearance signals lore, consequences, and reality-bending choices.

Islands, Farmworld, and the Edges

  • Islands: glimpse of pre-war humanity and tech; reshapes Finn's identity.
  • Farmworld: alternate reality spawned by a wish; the Grass Sword lineage threads through it.
  • Odd dimensions: City of Thieves, Pillow World, and the Citadel deepen the weird and widen the map.
